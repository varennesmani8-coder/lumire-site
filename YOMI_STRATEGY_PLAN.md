## Méthodologie Yomi Denzel

Yomi's approach combines:
1. **Trend Research** - Identify trending products via TikTok/Instagram (viral niche identification)
2. **Margin Analysis** - Calculate dropshipping viability (200%+ minimum margin required)
3. **Sourcing Validation** - Verify supplier availability and lead times
4. **Target Demographic** - Focus on women 18-45, beauty/wellness/lifestyle
5. **UGC Strategy** - Plan influencer/user-generated content campaigns
